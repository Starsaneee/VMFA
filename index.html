Featured Artists & Artifacts

Explore highlights from VMFA's permanent collection of more than 50,000 works.

Photography

Dapper

Jamel Shabazz · 1980

One of the most beloved street photographers of the hip-hop era, Jamel Shabazz documented the style and dignity of Black New Yorkers with warmth and intimacy.

Mixed Media · Sculpture

Vessel

Radcliffe Bailey · 2012

Atlanta-based artist Radcliffe Bailey creates multimedia works that weave together personal memory, African diaspora history, and cosmic imagery.

Oil on Canvas · Impressionism

Field of Poppies, Giverny

Claude Monet · 1885

A cornerstone of the Paul and Rachel Mellon Collection, this radiant Impressionist landscape captures the gardens at Giverny in full bloom.

Decorative Arts · Goldsmith

Imperial Peter the Great Easter Egg

Fabergé firm · 1903

One of only 50 Imperial Easter Eggs ever created by the House of Fabergé, presented by Tsar Nicholas II to Empress Alexandra in 1903.

Painting · Contemporary

Stadia III

Julie Mehretu · 2004

Ethiopian-American artist Julie Mehretu is celebrated for her monumental abstract works that layer architectural diagrams, maps, and gestural mark-making.

Collage · Mixed Media

Let Them Be Children

Deborah Roberts · 2018

Austin-based artist Deborah Roberts creates large-scale collages that center Black childhood, joy, and innocence.
